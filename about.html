Who am I and what is this page?

Hello! My name is Lindsay Neher, I'm 16 years old and I live in Idaho. I built this website for a school project, and I decided to incorporate my passion into it. I love to do many activites that involve horses. I compete in both Western and English events. I love to barrel race, goat tie, pole bend, train mustangs, and compete in jumpers. I have been riding and training horses for 8 years, I plan on doing it for many more!
