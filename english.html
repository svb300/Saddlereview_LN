Butet

Butet saddles are very high quality, and classy looking. These saddle last long term, and are extremly comfortable and help aid your riding position. They are made out of genuine leather and have an extremly nice fit.

Stubben

Stubben saddles have a very nice look, and last long term. They are made out of leather and have a nice fit to most horses. Depending on the style, Stubbens are either extremly comfortable or can cause discomfort.

Wintec

Wintec english saddles are synthetic, but nice looking. These saddles fall on the inexpensive side of English saddles, while still maintaining good quality. They are very comfortable saddles, and easy to clean. They last fairly long. However, the fit on these saddles varies. Make sure to check proportions.

My Opinion:

I currently own a Stubben saddle and it is not my favorite. It fits my horse well, but it is very uncomfortable for me to ride in. I find the seat to be very stiff and I get pain from working in it. Its not terrible, but I would prefer a different saddle. My favorite english saddle brand is Butet. They are extremly comfortable, fit will, and help aid my riding. Unfortuanatley they are extremly expensive, but I would definitely invest in one.
